Attorney Profile

Before moving to the Roanoke Valley, Nanda Davis was an attorney at the U.S. Department of Labor, in Washington, D.C., where she worked closely with two administrative law judges, writing their decisions and orders regarding a wide variety of employment related statutes.

Nanda Davis graduated magna cum laude from George Mason University school of law in 2012. During law school she worked at the law school’s domestic relations clinic, helping individuals who could not afford attorneys get uncontested divorces. She was also on the school’s national moot court team. Prior to graduating from law school she worked in a variety of legal settings, including Bernhard, Gardner & Mickelsen (a criminal and family law practice), and the chambers of the Honorable Stanley P. Klein, of the Fairfax County Circuit Court.

She lives in Salem with her husband and golden retriever mix.
