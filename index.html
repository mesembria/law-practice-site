Family

A divorce is a time of crisis for the whole family, but there is life on the other side. You need an objective lawyer who will listen to what you want, and guide you through the legal system to make sure your interests are being protected.
Divorce * Child Custody * Child Support * Alimony * Property Division * Pension Division

Criminal

Being charged with a crime can be terrifying, or it may make you angry. Maybe this is your first time dealing with the police. You need a lawyer who is ready to go to court to stand up for your rights.
Drug crimes * Assault * Theft * Fraud * Probation Violation

Traffic

Traffic convictions can have serious consequences, causing high insurance premiums, fines, suspension of your license, possibly jail time, and in some cases can cause you to lose your job. You need a lawyer to help you get the best possible outcome.
DUI/DWI * Reckless Driving * Speeding * Driving on Suspended
